Our Story

Founded in 2001, Raj Industries has grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of premium wire harnesses serving automotive, industrial, and aerospace sectors globally.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build long-lasting partnerships with clients worldwide. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver superior products.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a team of experienced engineers, we consistently exceed industry standards and customer expectations.
